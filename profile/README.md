## About My Little Puppy

My Little Puppy is a casual simulation game where players take care of a virtual pet. You can feed, train, and interact with your puppy while building a bond and watching it grow over time.

The game provides a relaxing environment where players can customize their puppy, decorate its space, and participate in fun activities. It is designed for users who enjoy light, interactive gameplay focused on care and creativity.
